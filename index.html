KERALA

  • Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country," is a popular tourism destination in India known for its natural beauty, backwaters, and unique cultural traditions.
  • Backwaters : Kerala's extensive network of serene backwaters is a major draw for tourists, offering a chance to cruise on traditional houseboats and take in the tranquil surroundings.
  • Hill Stations : The scenic hill stations of Munnar, Wayanad, and Thekkady are popular tourist destinations, offering picturesque landscapes and lush tea plantations.
  • Wildlife Sanctuaries : Kerala's wildlife sanctuaries, including Periyar National Park and Silent Valley National Park, offer a chance to spot exotic animals and birds.

SHIMLA

  • Shimla is a charming hill station located in the northern Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. Known for its picturesque landscapes and colonial architecture, Shimla is a popular tourist destination that attracts visitors from around the world.
  • Mall Road : This bustling street is the heart of Shimla and a popular tourist destination, lined with shops, restaurants, and cafes.
  • The Ridge : The Ridge is a large open space in the center of Shimla, offering panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Christ Church : This historic church, located on the Ridge, is one of Shimla's most iconic landmarks and a popular tourist destination

KOLKATA - The City of Joy

  • Kolkata, formerly known as Calcutta, is a vibrant and historic city located in the eastern part of India. As the capital of the state of West Bengal, Kolkata is known for its rich cultural heritage, colonial architecture, and delicious cuisine.
  • Victoria Memorial : This iconic landmark is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Kolkata, with its stunning architecture and historical significance.
  • Howrah Bridge : This famous bridge, built over the Hooghly River, is an engineering marvel and a popular spot for photography and sightseeing.
  • Dakshineswar Kali Temple : This famous temple, dedicated to the goddess Kali, is a popular pilgrimage site and a beautiful example of Bengali temple architecture.
